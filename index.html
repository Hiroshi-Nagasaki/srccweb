ABOUT THE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

Exponentially growing volumes of data, analyzed by increasingly intelligent machines are unlocking new possibilities and enabling solutions to challenges once deemed insurmountable. Advances in Artificial Intelligence (AI) have led to transformative applications across key sectors such as agriculture, healthcare, trade, transport and education. In the realm of digital trade, AI is revolutionizing global supply chains by making them smarter and more agile, thereby facilitating seamless cross-border transactions. At the same time, AI-driven green innovation is accelerating the transition to a low-carbon economy‚Äîthrough energy optimisation in industries and the advancement of circular economies via waste tracking and sustainable resource management.

The transformative potential of AI depends fundamentally on workforce skilling and equitable access to technology. As automation widens the digital skills gap, reskilling initiatives are crucial for cultivating an AI-augmented workforce. Equally important is the establishment of robust governance frameworks to address pressing ethical concerns such as data privacy, algorithmic bias and monopolistic control.

The theme of this conference, ‚ÄúDriving Green Innovation, Workforce Skilling, Digital Trade, Transformation and Governance‚Äù, emphasizes the role of AI in accelerating economic progress that is fundamentally "human-centric‚Äîprioritizing inclusivity and equitable distribution". This vision of growth extends beyond efficiency to place people and planetary well-being at the heart of technological advancement.

To embrace the transformative potential of AI, Shri Ram College of Commerce University of Delhi, under the aegis of its Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC, SRCC), in collaboration with KPR College of Arts, Science & Research, Coimbatore, and Churchill Institute, Australia, is organizing this International Conference on ‚ÄúAI for Sustainable Economic Growth‚Äù.

CALL FOR RESEARCH AND ACADEMIC CONTRIBUTIONS

Recognizing the profound implications of Artificial Intelligence for contemporary society, this conference invites research scholars, faculty members and postgraduate students to submit original scholarly work that engages critically with the conference theme. Submissions are encouraged on innovative applications of AI in sectors such as business, healthcare, and public policy, as well as on the ethical, social and developmental dimensions of AI integration. The conference aims to foster rigorous interdisciplinary dialogue that advances responsible, inclusive and sustainable pathways for AI-driven transformation.
